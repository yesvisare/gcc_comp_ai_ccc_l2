Sample RAG Queries for Audit Logging Testing
============================================

Financial Queries (SOX-Relevant, Confidential)
-----------------------------------------------
What were the Q4 2024 revenue figures for the EMEA region?
Show me all transactions over $1M in December 2024
What is the current status of our accounts receivable?
Generate a summary of capital expenditures for fiscal year 2024
Compare revenue growth year-over-year for product line A

HR Queries (PII-Sensitive, Internal)
-------------------------------------
What is the average tenure of software engineers?
Show me the organizational structure for the engineering department
What benefits are available to remote employees?
Summarize performance review feedback themes
What is the headcount growth plan for Q1 2025?

Legal Queries (Restricted, Legal Hold)
---------------------------------------
Summarize pending litigation cases
What are our obligations under the ACME vendor contract?
Show me all NDAs signed in 2024
What is our liability exposure for product XYZ?
Review compliance requirements for GDPR Article 17

Analytics Queries (Internal, Public)
-------------------------------------
What is the average customer satisfaction score?
Show me user engagement trends for the mobile app
What are the top 5 feature requests from customers?
Analyze churn rate patterns by customer segment
What is the conversion rate for the latest marketing campaign?

System Operations (Internal)
-----------------------------
What is the current database replication lag?
Show me API error rates for the past 24 hours
What is the average query latency for RAG operations?
List all scheduled maintenance windows for this month
What is the current disk usage for audit log storage?

Access Control Scenarios (Audit Required)
------------------------------------------
ALLOW: Finance analyst accessing financial reports
DENY: Contractor requesting access to confidential data
ALLOW: Legal counsel accessing litigation documents
DENY: Engineer attempting to access employee PII
ALLOW: Executive accessing cross-departmental analytics
DENY: External auditor requesting system credentials
ALLOW: Data scientist querying anonymized customer data
DENY: Temporary employee accessing restricted compliance data

Error Scenarios (Error Audit)
------------------------------
Vector database timeout after 30 seconds
LLM API rate limit exceeded - retrying with backoff
Document retrieval failed - insufficient access permissions
Audit log write failed - database connection lost
Hash chain verification failed - potential tampering detected
S3 archival failed - bucket permissions insufficient
SIEM integration timeout - Splunk HEC unreachable

Compliance Flag Examples
-------------------------
SOX_RELEVANT: Financial data access requiring audit trail
PII: Personally identifiable information accessed
MNPI: Material non-public information (insider trading concern)
LEGAL_HOLD: Data subject to legal preservation order
HIPAA_PHI: Protected health information (healthcare context)
PCI_DSS: Payment card data access
EXPORT_CONTROL: Data subject to export restrictions
ATTORNEY_CLIENT: Attorney-client privileged communication

Multi-Tenant Scenarios
----------------------
Finance tenant: Quarterly earnings analysis
HR tenant: Headcount planning
Legal tenant: Contract review
IT tenant: Infrastructure monitoring
Analytics tenant: Customer behavior analysis
Marketing tenant: Campaign performance
Sales tenant: Pipeline forecasting
Product tenant: Feature usage analytics

Retention Policy Test Cases
----------------------------
Hot storage (0-90 days): Recent queries in PostgreSQL
Warm storage (91-365 days): Recent year in S3 Standard
Cold storage (366-2555 days): 7-year archive in S3 Glacier
Deletion prohibited: Cannot delete any audit logs
Archival only: Admin can move to cold storage, never delete
Compliance verification: Annual audit trail integrity check
